HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER

Pagawa is a professional essay writing service that cares about quality of each output delivered to our clients. If you strive for excellent results, provide as much information about the project as possible in order to receive the best services. Tell us the title, goal, needed solutions, number of pages, deadline, formatting style and number of sources for citation. Be attentive, attach full requirements from college professor or copy and paste them into the order form. Preferably, send your scoring rubric, lecture notes or presentations, textbooks or any other useful materials. On this stage, a lot depends on you, as well as cooperation with your personal assistant. Don’t hesitate over sending guidelines after order was placed, better late than never!

WRITING/EDITING/SOLVING PROCESS IN A NUTSHELL

Writers/Editors/Scholars at Pagawa are devoted to what they do and have an inbuilt process of completing any order. Here’s how the process is usually organized:

1.) After accepting your project, writer checks all instructions, ensures nothing valuable was missed.
2.) They get familiar with topic - do research on the Internet, find scholarly sources from credible databases, read them thoroughly and makes notes.


3.) Writers create an outline along with thesis for themselves, so that final paper is organized, smooth and has a flow. Editors create a draft for themselves with the contents so that the final product is organized, smooth, and aesthetically pleasing. Scholars create a scratch sheet to organize, succinct, and propagate information.
4.) Next step is drafting paper from scratch, implementing sources, incorporating evidence, editing pictures, answering questions. You may ask for a draft by messaging an employee, checking it or providing some comments for improvement. 
5.) At the end, writer/editor/scholar produces final variant, cites all used sources, proofreads, also edits the text before delivering it, saves the product in the right format, concisely fniishes the task with all the needed information present. If for some reason you are not satisfied with an assignment, there is an opportunity to request free unlimited revisions or ask another editor for improvements. Alternatively, in accordance with our customer satisfaction policy, you may request a refund, if you think that instructions were not followed.
